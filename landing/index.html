The Story

Ku Ke Ri are species with only one purpose defining their existence - SLAY THE DEMONS ON EARTH!

Once a year they come down to the planet of the humans to perform their ritual of cleansing, banishing the evil spirits back to their demonic realm. This ritual of blood and strife is not an easy one for the Ku Ke Ri race. Throughout the millennia, they suffered terrible losses giving the demons the chance to possess and corrupt the peaceful human race and turn them into angry, selfish and corrupt beings. Our saviours did not despair, however. They organized a grand tournament, a feat of strength and will that would prepare them for the next fight with the demons. It is up to you to prepare them for the great battle ahead.

The Gameplay

Ku Ke RI is an online multiplayer only game created to serve one purpose - dominate your opponents. The game achieves this through randomization, ensuring high replayability and fun without repetitiveness.
